General info about iOS 13

There have been a few reports about touch ID no longer working after an iPhone screen repair on iOS 13. So far I have repaired 10+ (will be outdated) phones and this has only happened once to me (and there was visible water damage in this phone so it's hard to pin-point the issue).

Batteries
